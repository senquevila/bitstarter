CrowdBlood is a mobile application, used to show others the posibility of sharing life by donating blood.

Initialy through the power and distribution of smartphones, CrowdBlood will let you ask for a blood donation or help others that match your blood type, and are near your location by donating the powerful gift that is your blood.

In many countries blood donation's culture is inexistent, meanwhile people needs blood. An accident, a surgery or even a disease is something no one is safe, no matter there social status, religion, ethnic or age. So we join this necessity with the power of technology to make things happen.

We try to make a comunity that wants to stay connected to help people near them, give them a second chance to live, who understand how powerful is donating blood the help of the Red Cross and Hospitals.

FAQ

Why do we need money?
CrowdBlood like any other application, needs funds to maintain the services up and ready and show other people this amazing app.
Where can I use Crowdblood?
CrowdBlood is based in a close comunity, so if you want create a comunity in your city we can help to announce to other people near of you.
Is this comunity safe?
CrowdBlood connect a need of blood donation with a possible donators. This donation is realized in well known institutions like Red Cross or hospitals.
